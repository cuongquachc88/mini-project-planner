Features & Roadmap

Mini Project Planner — offline-first, multi-project, PWA

Overview

🎯

Who it's for

Solo devs or small teams who want full project management without SaaS lock-in or monthly fees.

📱

Offline-first PWA

Install on desktop or mobile. All data lives locally in PGlite. Works 100% without internet.

☁️

Optional Drive Sync

Back up to Google Drive on demand or every 15 minutes. One SQL file, no backend needed.

Getting Started

  1. 1Open the app at / and click the install button in your browser address bar to install as a PWA.
  2. 2Visit Profile to set your name and email. You'll be set as the admin.
  3. 3Create your first project from the home screen. Pick a color and key (e.g. PROJ).
  4. 4Optionally connect Google Drive from the top bar to enable backup sync.
